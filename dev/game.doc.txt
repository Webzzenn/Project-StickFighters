Summary
Project stickfighters is a platform fighter inspired by video games like "the strongest battlegrounds" , "smash bros" , "stick fight animations"
the game has some unique mechanics that makes it stand out from other similar projects.

Backend Mechanics

local player, up to 4 devices
peer to peer multiplayer, 1v1 2v2

controller support
keyboard support

Gameplay mechanics

moveset Building
 at the start of each game you'll be able to pick a meele style, 4 abilities, two curses and two wishes.

 Meele Style
   Meele uses and purpose
   Meeles are avaliable at all times and have no long cooldown, they are used to start combos and keep your enemy stunned
   Meeles have a set number of "punches" before having to wait for them to reset, the last meele punch will always do knockback
  Styles

     an all rounded style,  
     4 total "punches"
     about 0.25s per punch
     reset cooldown: 1 second

     a slower but more powerful style,
     3 total "punches"
     0.35s per punch
     last punch is powerful
     reset cooldown: 1 second

     a quicker but less powerful style,
     5 total "punches"
     0.17s per punch
     last punch isn't incredibly strong
     reset cooldown: 1 second

     a mix of slow and strong,
     5 total "Punches"
     0.26s for the first 2 punches 0.33s for third, 0.35s for fourth 
     reset cooldown: 1 second

 Wishes
    Wishes are essentially passive perks. for example, jump slightly higher when on lower health
